# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project showcases an **Interactive E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** using the **Sample Superstore** dataset. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), analyze sales trends, evaluate regional performance, and identify top-performing products and customers.

The dashboard is fully interactive with **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Timelines**, allowing users to filter and explore the data dynamically.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

✅  Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
✅  Identify top-performing products and customers.
✅  Compare sales across regions and customer segments.
✅  Monitor monthly sales trends.
✅  Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability.
✅  Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Technology is the highest revenue-generating product category.
* Furniture records comparatively lower profit due to higher discounts.
* The West region contributes the highest overall sales.
* Consumer customers generate the largest share of revenue.
* Sales peak during November and December, indicating strong seasonal demand.
* Higher discount percentages often lead to reduced profit margins.
